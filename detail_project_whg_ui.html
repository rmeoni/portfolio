Wind Hill Growers UI Design
Client
Wind Hill Growers
Release
13/11/2021


As a UX designer, I expanded this beautiful brand's website and built a Design System in Figma to keep all digital products on-brand. Collaborating with the team, I've continued to enhance the Squarespace site, and created a store to take orders through Zoho.

The Problem

When I joined Wind Hill Growers, they only had a public-facing website. However, they were taking orders via email and managing everything through Excel. They wanted a wholesaler store that would hide prices and display them only to invited users.

The challenge was that their Squarespace website showcased their brand but lacked a Design System. I started by creating a Design System to maintain brand consistency and establish a single source of truth. Then, I developed a webstore using Zoho to allow wholesalers to place orders. The Design System brought the brand into consistency, and the Zoho webstore streamlined online ordering processes.

The Goal

The goal was to create an online webstore that would hide prices and display them only to invited wholesalers while maintaining brand consistency.

My Role

As the UX Designer and Developer, I handled the project from start to finish:

  • Design: I created a Design System to unify the brand and serve as a single source of truth. I designed Figma prototypes for the online store and developed them using Zoho.
  • Development: The store was built using Zoho Commerce. To adhere to the brand's guidelines, I used extensive Custom CSS to customize the pre-made template.
  • Functionality: Once the webstore adhered to the brand guidelines, the company started accepting online orders through Zoho Commerce. This enabled automated reporting, streamlined processes, and better stock management.

Responsibilities

  • Created a Design System for the brand to ensure cohesion using Figma and Zeroheight.
  • Designed the UI in Figma to align with the brand’s identity.
  • Developed the site in Zoho Commerce using Custom CSS to adhere to the brand's guidelines.

The final product enabled Wind Hill Growers to take online orders while maintaining a consistent brand across all platforms and delivering a great customer experience.
